We Help You Apply for the Right Commercial Trucking Insurance — Fast.

Whether you’re a new owner‑operator or an experienced carrier, commercial insurance is a legal requirement and one of the first steps to start hauling loads. We’ll help you submit the right way — with speed, accuracy, and trusted partners.

Why Truck Insurance Matters:

  • Required by FMCSA to activate your authority
  • Mandatory to haul freight under most brokers and shippers
  • Often required by ports and local work with warehouses
  • Protects your driver, business reputation, and financial security

What We Offer for $399:

  • One‑day insurance application assistance — we guide everything needed to get covered fast
  • Help choosing the right coverage for your business and authority
  • Work with insurance agents who understand trucking and commercial needs
  • Guidance on common legal requirements (certs, filings, cargo and liability)
  • Preparation tips to ensure approval and better pricing

Types of Insurance We Help You With:

  • Primary Auto Liability — required to activate your DOT authority
  • Cargo Insurance — covers goods in transit
  • General Liability — covers your business’s general risks
  • Non‑Trucking Liability (Bobtail) — when driving without a load
  • Physical Damage — protection for your tractor or trailer

Who Is This Service For?

  • New Authority / First‑Time Owner‑Operators
  • Lease‑on drivers switching into their own business
  • Fleet owners who are expanding or renewing policies
  • Anyone needing the right coverage to access better lanes

What You’ll Need:

  • Your CDL and business information
  • Basic details about your truck/trailer (help provided)
  • DOT and MC numbers (or we’ll help if not started)
  • Preferred lane needs and driving history

We Know What Insurers Look For

We package your application to be accurate, professional, and optimized for approval. We help you avoid rejections and delays that could cost your loads.

Let’s Get You Covered — Today

Apply once — and we’ll handle the rest. Focus on driving while we get you insured and legally road‑ready.
