Rented Trailer Program – $500 for 3 Months

Don’t have a trailer? No problem. We offer road‑ready trailers for rent so you can get moving immediately and start earning without the upfront cost of buying your own.

What You Get

  • A DOT‑Compliant Trailer — fully inspected and ready for immediate use
  • Choice of trailer type — Dry Van or Reefer (based on availability)
  • Maintenance support — we cover regular wear‑and‑tear maintenance
  • Flexible use — for dedicated lanes, Amazon loads, or spot freight
  • Legally registered & insured trailers — ready for dispatch fast

Who This Is For

  • New owner‑operators who haven’t purchased a trailer yet
  • Drivers with tractor‑only setups
  • Fleet owners needing additional capacity fast
  • Anyone who wants to test lanes before committing to trailer ownership

Why Our Trailer Rental Makes Sense

  • Low upfront cost — only $500 for 90 days of use
  • No long‑term contract — use it while you secure consistent loads
  • Avoid downtime — start hauling ASAP, even if your trailer is under repair
  • Better load access — some loads require specific trailer types; we help you qualify

Requirements to Qualify

  • Valid CDL and active authority (or working under ours)
  • Insurance coverage (we help you apply if needed)
  • Signed trailer lease agreement
  • Trailer must be returned in working condition or renewed

Add‑On Options Available

Ask us about: trailer tracking, additional coverage, reefer units, and expedited dispatch options.
