What Is a Dedicated Lane?

A dedicated lane is a pre-assigned, consistent freight route between the same pickup and delivery locations — typically running the same schedule each week. This is one of the most reliable ways for truck drivers and owner-operators to secure steady work and predictable income.

Benefits of a Dedicated Lane:

  • ✅ Steady Weekly Miles & Freight – No scrambling for last-minute loads
  • ✅ Consistent Income – Know what you'll earn before hitting the road
  • ✅ Preferred Routes – Choose lanes based on region, home time, or freight type
  • ✅ No Load Board Hassle – We handle the negotiations, paperwork & dispatch
  • ✅ Build Long-Term Shipper Relationships – Leading to even more opportunity
